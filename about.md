Hi, I'm Innes. I'm a software engineer and "data guy" over at
[Cocoon Labs][0] in Leeds.

Day to day I write Python/Go and a variety of other languages for our
cloud services and on device software along with generating automated reports,
improving developer tooling, creating visualisations and generally trying to
work functional programming concepts into anywhere I can find a place for them
in the codebase.

Outside of work I'm a photographer, amateur Physicist and Mathematician
([helping Dr John Williamson][1] with his theory of [Absolute Relativity][2])
and general nerd. I live in York with my amazing wife [Katie][3] and our two
wonderful/trouble making daughters.
